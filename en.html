Corporate Inquiry

Common questions.

How far in advance should I book?＋
7–10 days before departure is recommended; 10–14 days in peak season or for Southeast Asia routes. VIP rush service can go as short as 3–5 days — contact support to confirm.
How much does it cost?＋
Starting prices per bag, each way: Japan USD280, Malaysia USD313, Singapore USD313, Thailand USD391. Final cost depends on dates, delivery point, and insurance — quoted within 24 hours of booking.
How is the bag packed? Will it get damaged?＋
Our staff checks the packaging at pickup, adds impact protection, and photographs everything. We recommend club head covers; keep small valuables with you.
How do I track my bag?＋
Join our LINE updates after booking — we message you at every checkpoint: pickup, departure, customs, delivery. You also get an official tracking code.
What if it’s delayed or damaged?＋
Our support team steps in immediately: tracking the shipment, coordinating with the carrier, filing claims, or arranging backup. Compensation follows carrier terms and any added insurance.
Can you ship the return leg too?＋
Yes — just mention it when booking. When your trip ends, we ship your bag from the overseas course or hotel straight back to Taiwan.
Can I ship multiple bags (team trips)?＋
Yes — 3+ bags get a volume discount. Corporate teams and training camps can discuss annual contracts and custom rates.
